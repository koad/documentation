
# **Jason and the Amazing Technicolor Dream Bus**  


![image](https://cdn.koad.sh/magic.png)


### **A Journey into the Mind**  

Life is a journey, and you are riding a **technicolor bus** through time. This bus is your **consciousness**, carrying you forward, moment by moment, choice by choice. You might think you’re in control, but have you ever stopped to ask: **Who is really driving?**  

At the front, behind the wheel, sits **the driver**—your **inner child, your subconscious, your ego**. It’s doing its best to navigate the road ahead, using old maps and past experiences to avoid danger. It means well, but it is **fearful, reactive, and conditioned** by everything it has learned since birth. If it was taught that love is dangerous, it will steer away from love. If it was taught that success is unsafe, it will sabotage opportunities. It does not question—it only obeys its programming.  

Behind the driver sit **twelve passengers**—your **twelve disciples**, the faculties of your mind. Some are strong, others weak. Some are wise, others confused. Right now, they are all **shouting directions at the driver**, each with their own agenda, creating chaos instead of clarity. These disciples are not your enemies, but they must be trained.  

And then, at the very front of the bus, sits an **empty pilot seat**.  

This seat belongs to **Jesus—the Christ consciousness, the divine master within you**. But Jesus is not there yet. Right now, the seat is vacant because the disciples are not yet disciplined. The driver is still scared. The bus moves, but it does not move with purpose.  

### **The Goal of This Journey**  

This is not about **killing the ego**—the driver is your inner child, and you are its parent. The goal is to **teach the driver** that it is safe, that it does not need to be afraid. The goal is to **train the disciples** so they stop shouting and instead **report to Jesus**, the divine intelligence that will guide the journey with wisdom, grace, and power.  

When this happens, **the bus transforms**. It is no longer just a vehicle moving blindly through life—it becomes an **unstoppable force, a chariot of light, capable of taking you anywhere under the grace of God**.  

But first, you must understand the **rules of the road**.
