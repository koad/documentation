ZeroTier + VNC/RDP changed how I think about machines.

Instead of needing a powerful laptop, I just need a **window** into my home system.

---

### ZeroTier: Hole-Punching Heaven

- **No open ports**
- **Private overlay**
- **Auto-connected mesh**
- **Works anywhere** (hotel wifi, LTE tether, etc.)

I don’t expose **any** public IPs. I remote in through a full VPN mesh that I control.

---

### Throwaway Client, Permanent Server

My $50 throwaway laptop:

- Stores *zero* data
- Boots fast, runs light
- Becomes a terminal into a high-powered desktop in my garage

If it dies, no problem — just grab another and go.

---

### Pure Sovereignty

> Don’t bring your data to the cloud.  
> Bring your screen to your data.

This model gives me **total sovereignty**:  
- I own the metal  
- I own the network  
- I own the software  

Nothing leaks, nothing tracks. Full power, anywhere I roam.
