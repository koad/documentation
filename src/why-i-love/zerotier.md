It's like having my own LAN stretched across the whole planet — no port forwarding, no firewalls to beg access from, just direct secure connections.

---

### Holepunching Done Right

- NAT traversal just works
- End-to-end encryption
- Local IPs across all machines
- Control plane can be self-hosted

No cloud dependency if you don't want it.

---

### My Setup

- **MPD in the garage**  
- **Beatify extensions on mobile**  
- **Desktops and laptops all meshed**  
- **Kids' entities with parental control**  
- **DDP + MongoDB over ZeroTier**

Everything can reach everything — but **only** what I allow.

---

### Why It Matters

ZeroTier allows **total topology control**. I define the mesh. I don’t trust ISPs, I don’t trust routers, I don’t trust cloud VPN providers. I trust **math and code**.

> Sovereignty starts with the network layer.  
> ZeroTier is where mine begins.
