### Sovereign Identity, Extended :heart:

Beatify lets me:

- Extend my PGP key with virtually any social/network/device proof
- Create a portable "link-in-bio" type profile, cryptographically signed
- Use my **PHP key** as a universal identity layer across the web
- Prove Who I am, and where to buy my wares, or sponsor my work.

> It's not just about identity — it's about trust, and it's mine to own.

---

### Integrated Into the Browser

- Browser extension: travels with me, cross-platform
- Lets me **log/rate** and **pin** content, pages, people — anything I browse
- Connects directly to my Beatify Desktop or koad:io-daemon

---

### Social Proof + Media Memory

* Works with my **Groove Basin** media logs + MPD activity
* Tracks media I consume across platforms (YouTube, Spotify, Local, etc.)
* Stores everything **locally** (not some 3rd party cloud)

---

### A Keybase Companion

* Built to include/use **existing Keybase PGP** or **bash/gpg** infrastructure
* But extends into *more abstract* account proofs, event emitters and *richer* metadata
* My Beatify Profile is my *real* public profile: decentralized, extensible

---

## Be Blessed

**Beatify** as in *to declare blessed* → love, uplift, elevate.

It’s perfect for a social identity layer with sovereignty + intention. The use of *“Beatify someone”* as a verb is both symbolic **and** system-ready.
