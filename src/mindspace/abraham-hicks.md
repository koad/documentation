# Abraham-Hicks

**💫 Tuning Into the Frequency of Creation**

If you've ever heard the phrase **“you create your own reality”**, you've already touched the edges of the Abraham-Hicks teachings. Channeled by **Esther Hicks**, *Abraham* is not a person, but a collective non-physical consciousness that speaks with clarity, humor, and radical permission to feel good.

Their message? Simple and revolutionary:

> **“You are a vibrational being in a vibrational universe. And you are always attracting.”**

---

### The Law of Attraction, Reframed

At the heart of the Abraham teachings is the **Law of Attraction**—not as a gimmick, but as a **cosmic law** as real and reliable as gravity:

> **Like attracts like.**
> **You get what you feel.**
> **Your emotional state is your point of attraction.**

In other words: your mood is a magnet. It doesn't matter what you *say* you want—what matters is what you're *broadcasting*.

---

### The 3-Step Creative Process

1. **Ask** – Your contrast (challenges) causes you to launch new desires.
2. **It Is Given** – The universe *immediately* answers and creates it vibrationally.
3. **Allow** – Your only task is to align with it—by feeling like it's already done.

> “You don’t need to *make* it happen. You need to get out of the way and *let* it happen.”

This isn’t laziness—it’s about learning to **trust your inner guidance system** (aka your emotions) as your direct line to Source.

---

### Emotional Guidance = Spiritual GPS

Abraham doesn't preach morality, sacrifice, or effort. They teach **emotional navigation**:

* Joy, love, enthusiasm = alignment
* Doubt, fear, frustration = resistance

> **Your job is to feel your way forward.**

Not because emotions are “woo”—but because they are **precision indicators of your current vibrational stance**.

---

### The Vortex

Abraham speaks often of “The Vortex”—their name for the vibrational reality where all your desires already exist.

You don’t create your desires through effort—you tune to them by:

* Relaxing,
* Trusting,
* Appreciating,
* Allowing.

As you align with joy, you become a **match to what you want**. Not because you earned it, but because you became available to receive it.

---

### Why It Resonates Now

In a world addicted to struggle, Abraham teaches **ease without avoidance**.
In a culture addicted to control, they teach **trust without passivity**.
In a time of information overload, they invite you back to **your own vibration** as your compass.

---

### Final Thought

Esther Hicks doesn't "pretend" to be spiritual. She channels it—literally. But even if you don't believe in channeling, **the teachings stand on their own**:

> “You are the creator of your own reality.
> You came here for joy.
> And there’s nothing serious going on here.”

So relax. Feel good. Focus on what you want. And let the Universe do the heavy lifting.

**That’s alignment. That’s sovereignty. That’s Abraham.**
