# Edgar Cayce

**🔮 The Sleeping Prophet: Medical Clairvoyance and Soul Records**

Edgar Cayce (1877-1945) was America's most documented psychic and spiritual healer. Known as **"The Sleeping Prophet,"** Cayce would enter a self-induced trance state and provide detailed diagnoses and treatments for illnesses he had no medical knowledge of, along with profound spiritual insights into the nature of the soul, reincarnation, and human purpose.

A Sunday School teacher and photographer by trade, Cayce was initially bewildered by his abilities. Over his lifetime, he gave more than **14,000 psychic readings** that were meticulously documented and verified, covering everything from medical diagnoses to past life regressions, ancient civilizations, and future predictions.

---

## The Cayce Phenomenon

### How Cayce Worked

Cayce's process was unique and scientifically documented:

1. **Preparation** - Removed shoes, loosened clothing, lay on couch
2. **Induction** - Entered self-hypnotic state with voice guidance
3. **Trance State** - Eyes closed, voice speaking from "unconscious"
4. **Connection** - Connected to universal consciousness/Akashic records
5. **Reading** - Provided detailed information while asleep
6. **Waking** - Remembered nothing of what was said

**Remarkable aspect:** Cayce himself had no formal medical training yet provided sophisticated diagnoses and treatments that often baffled physicians.

The readings taught that **reincarnation is real and purposeful**:

- **Life Lessons** - Each lifetime addresses specific spiritual needs
- **Free Will** - We can change patterns through conscious choice

### 3. The Mind-Body Connection

Cayce emphasized that **most illness begins in the mind**:

- **Thought Patterns** Create Physical Symptoms
- **Emotional Blocks** Cause Energy Imbalances
- **Spiritual Neglect** Leads to Physical Disease
- **Holistic Healing** Requires Addressing All Levels

### 4. The Universal Laws

Key spiritual laws that govern reality:

- **Like Attracts Like** - Vibration determines manifestation
- **What You Sow, You Reap** - Karma operates in all areas
- **As Above, So Below** - Microcosm reflects macrocosm
- **Energy Follows Thought** - Consciousness creates reality

- **Relationship Patterns** Why certain people are in our lives

Profound metaphysical teachings:

## Essential Cayce Concepts

### The Body Temple

Cayce taught that the body is a **sacred temple** requiring proper care:

- **Diet is Key** - "Let your food be your medicine"
- **Elimination** - Regular cleansing and detoxification
- **Spinal Alignment** - Osteopathy and chiropractic care
- **Energy Balance** - Keeping the body's electrical system healthy
- **Rest and Meditation** - Essential for physical and spiritual health

### The Ideal Diet

The readings consistently recommended:

- **80% Alkaline Foods** - Fresh fruits and vegetables
- **20% Acid Foods** - Whole grains, proteins
- **No Fried Foods** - Avoid unhealthy fats
- **No Pork** - Specifically mentioned many times
- **Plenty of Water** - Pure, clean water daily

### The Power of Prayer and Meditation

Spiritual practices for healing and growth:

- **Affirmative Prayer** - Speaking truth with conviction
- **Daily Meditation** - Quiet the mind to hear Spirit
- **Healing Attunements** - Using prayer for others
- **Dream Work** - Understanding messages from the subconscious
- **Gratitude Practice** - Cultivating thankfulness daily

## Famous Cayce Remedies and Treatments

### The Castor Oil Pack

A simple yet powerful healing remedy:

- **Castor Oil** applied to flannel cloth
- **Heat Application** - Hot water bottle or heating pad
- **Placement** - Over liver, abdomen, or affected area
- **Duration** - 1-2 hours daily
- **Benefits** - Detoxification, inflammation reduction, healing

### The Atomidine

An iodine-based solution:

- **Thyroid Support** - Regulates metabolism
- **Detoxification** - Cleanses the system
- **Immune Enhancement** - Boosts natural defenses
- **Cellular Regeneration** - Promotes healing

### The Wet Cell Battery

Electrical healing device:

- **Mild Electrical Current** - Stimulates healing
- **Vibrational Medicine** - Balances body energy
- **Nerve Regeneration** - Helps with neurological conditions
- **Pain Relief** - Reduces various types of pain
