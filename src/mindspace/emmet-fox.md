# Emmet Fox

**🔥 The Mental Science: Transforming Life Through Mind**

Emmet Fox (1886-1951) was a New Thought spiritual leader, author, and lecturer who became one of the most influential voices in practical Christianity and mental science. His groundbreaking work, **"The Sermon on the Mount,"** introduced millions to the practical power of Jesus' teachings as a complete system for mental transformation.

Originally trained as an electrical engineer, Fox discovered New Thought principles while recovering from illness. He became a minister at the Divine Science Church in New York, where his weekly lectures attracted thousands seeking practical spiritual solutions to life's problems.

---

## Core Teaching: The Mental Interpretation of Scripture

Fox's revolutionary contribution was demonstrating that **the Bible is not historical but psychological**—a complete manual for training the mind. He taught that Jesus' statements are scientific laws of consciousness that anyone can apply.

### The Seven Main Laws

1. **The Law of Thought** - Thoughts create your reality
2. **The Law of Belief** - What you believe becomes true for you
3. **The Law of Expectation** - You get what you expect
4. **The Law of Substitution** - Replace negative thoughts with positive ones
5. **The Law of Relaxation** - Mental and physical ease enhances power
6. **The Law of Forgiveness** - Releasing resentment heals your mind
7. **The Law of Non-Resistance** - What you fight, you strengthen

## Key Concepts

### Scientific Prayer

Fox redefined prayer as **mental treatment**—the systematic changing of consciousness:

- **Denials** - Mentally reject false beliefs about yourself
- **Affirmations** - Declare truth about your divine nature
- **Treatment** - Systematic mental work on specific issues
- **Realization** - Deep conviction that transforms consciousness

### The Seven-Day Mental Diet

Fox's most famous practical technique:

- **Never entertain a negative thought** for more than a few seconds
- **Replace immediately** with its spiritual opposite
- **Practice for 7 days** without interruption
- **Start over** if you slip during the period
- **Life-changing results** when consistently practiced

### The One Power

"There is only one power, and that is God. There is only one presence, and that is God. There is only one activity, and that is God."

This realization eliminates all fear, worry, and limitation when truly understood and practiced.

## Essential Books and Their Messages

### *The Sermon on the Mount: The Key to Success in Life* (1934)

**The Masterpiece: Complete Manual for Mental Transformation**

Fox's most famous work provides a line-by-line interpretation of Jesus' most profound teaching as practical mental science.

**Key teachings:**
- The Beatitudes as states of consciousness
- How to "seek first the kingdom" (consciousness)
- Practical application of "turn the other cheek"
- The scientific meaning of "resist not evil"
- How to manifest abundance through spiritual laws
- The true meaning of prayer and meditation

### *The Mental Equivalent* (1943)

**The Method: Creating Mental Equivalents for Desired Outcomes**

A practical guide to creating the mental conditions that produce desired results in life.

**Key teachings:**
- How to construct mental equivalents
- The relationship between thoughts and material conditions
- Building consciousness for health, wealth, and happiness
- Overcoming limiting mental patterns
- The mechanics of creative thinking

### *The Ten Commandments* (1945)

**The Foundation: Understanding Divine Laws as Mental Principles**

Fox interprets each commandment as a mental law that governs consciousness and experience.

**Key teachings:**
- Each commandment as a mental principle
- How breaking these laws creates suffering
- The psychological meaning of "thou shalt not"
- Practical application for modern life
- Freedom through understanding divine law

### *Power Through Constructive Thinking* (1932)

**The Application: Practical Guidance for Daily Life**

One of Fox's earliest works, providing practical techniques for transforming everyday consciousness.

**Key teachings:**
- How to think constructively about problems
- Building positive mental habits
- The power of faith and expectation
- Overcoming fear and worry
- Creating success through right thinking

## Fox's Practical Techniques

### The Practice of the Presence

**Continuous awareness of God's presence:**

- **Morning Affirmation** - "God is with me today"
- **Mid-day Check-in** - Realign with divine presence
- **Evening Gratitude** - Thank God for the day's blessings
- **Crisis Response** - Instantly turn to divine presence

### The Forgiveness Method

**Systematic release of resentment:**

1. **List** everyone who has wronged you
2. **Bless** each person mentally
3. **Forgive** completely and unconditionally
4. **Release** all desire for punishment
5. **Free** yourself from the burden

### The Prosperity Method

**Building consciousness for abundance:**

- **Daily Affirmations** - "I am abundantly supplied"
- **Visualization** - See yourself already prosperous
- **Gratitude Practice** - Thank God for present and future abundance
- **Generosity** - Give freely, knowing you receive
- **Expectation** - Anticipate good coming your way

## Fox's Interpretation of Key Bible Passages

### "Resist not evil" (Matthew 5:39)

**Fox's meaning:** Don't give negative thoughts any attention or energy. Starve them of focus and they die from neglect.

### "Seek ye first the kingdom" (Matthew 6:33)

**Fox's meaning:** Seek consciousness first (the kingdom within), and all material things will be added automatically.

### "Judge not" (Matthew 7:1)

**Fox's meaning:** Don't criticize or condemn, because judgment creates the very conditions you're judging in yourself.

### "The kingdom of God is within you" (Luke 17:21)

**Fox's meaning:** Divine consciousness is your true nature, already present within you, not something to seek externally.
