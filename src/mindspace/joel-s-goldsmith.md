# Joel S. Goldsmith

**✨ The Infinite Way: Spiritual Mysticism for Modern Life**

Joel S. Goldsmith (1892-1963) was a spiritual teacher and mystic who brought forth a profound message of spiritual illumination that transcended traditional religion. His teaching, called **The Infinite Way**, offers a direct path to experiencing God as infinite consciousness—not as a distant deity, but as the very fabric of our being.

Goldsmith was a Christian Science practitioner who, through deep mystical experience, received a revelation that would become The Infinite Way. He taught that **spiritual truth is universal** and appears in all true religious traditions, though it's often hidden beneath layers of theology and dogma.

---

## Core Teachings of The Infinite Way

### 1. God Is Infinite Consciousness

Goldsmith taught that God is not a person, place, or thing—but **infinite consciousness, wisdom, love, and life**. This consciousness is not outside you; it is your true nature.

> "God is not a power that can be overcome by any other power. God is infinite, and infinity cannot be opposed."

### 2. Spiritual Healing Through Realization

Unlike approaches that try to manipulate outcomes, Goldsmith taught that **healing happens through the realization of our spiritual nature**. When we recognize that we live and move and have our being in God consciousness, the illusion of disease dissolves.

### 3. The Four-Stage Path

Goldsmith outlined a clear progression of spiritual development:

1. **Seeking** - The search for truth outside oneself
2. **Finding** - The inner discovery of spiritual truth
3. **Integration** - Living from this spiritual realization
4. **Impersonalization** - Serving as a conduit for divine consciousness

### 4. The Nature of Error (Sin)

Goldsmith redefined "sin" not as moral failure but as **the belief in separation from God**. This belief creates all forms of suffering, limitation, and discord. The solution isn't punishment but awakening to our unity with the divine.

## Key Books and Their Messages

### *The Infinite Way* (1947)

**The Foundation: Awakening to Spiritual Reality**

This is Goldsmith's foundational work, introducing the complete system of The Infinite Way. It establishes the core principles and provides the framework for understanding spiritual healing and consciousness.

**Key teachings:**
- The distinction between human and spiritual consciousness
- How to practice "the silence" (meditation)
- The principles of spiritual healing
- Understanding true prayer as communion, not petition

### *The Art of Meditation* (1955)

**The Practice: Entering the Silence**

Goldsmith considered meditation the cornerstone of spiritual practice. This book is a practical guide to moving from intellectual understanding to direct experience of divine consciousness.

**Key teachings:**
- Meditation as listening, not thinking
- Moving from human to spiritual awareness
- The relationship between meditation and daily life
- Overcoming obstacles in meditation practice

### *Practicing the Presence* (1958)

**The Integration: Living Spiritual Truth**

This book bridges the gap between meditation and daily living, showing how to maintain spiritual awareness in the midst of worldly activities.

**Key teachings:**
- How to maintain awareness of God's presence throughout the day
- Spiritual principles for work, relationships, and daily activities
- The importance of gratitude and appreciation
- Living from the "inner kingdom" rather than outer circumstances

---

## What Makes Goldsmith Unique

### 1. Universal Christianity

While coming from a Christian background, Goldsmith taught that **the Christ is the divine nature in all people**, not just one historical figure. His teaching honors the mystical core of Christianity while recognizing the same truth in all authentic spiritual paths.

### 2. Practical Mysticism

Goldsmith's teaching isn't about escaping the world but **transforming your experience of it**. He showed how to bring spiritual consciousness into business, relationships, health, and every aspect of human life.

### 3. No Techniques, Only Principles

Unlike many spiritual teachings that offer specific techniques, Goldsmith emphasized **living from principles rather than following formulas**. Each person must discover their own unique expression of the universal truth.

## Essential Goldsmith Quotes

> "The secret of the Infinite Way is that there is no secret. There is only the truth that you already are, and the truth that God already is."

> "You cannot overcome error with truth; you can only overcome error with the realization that there is no error."

> "The kingdom of God is not coming; it is here. Heaven is not a place you go to; it is a state of consciousness you awaken to."

> "Prayer is not asking God for something; it is opening yourself to receive what God is already giving."

## Why Goldsmith Matters Today

In an age of spiritual seeking often characterized by quick fixes and external solutions, Goldsmith offers something profound:

- **Authenticity** - His teaching comes from genuine mystical experience
- **Depth** - The Infinite Way provides a complete spiritual system
- **Universality** - The principles apply to anyone, regardless of background
- **Practicality** - The teachings transform daily life, not just meditation

Goldsmith bridges the gap between ancient mystical wisdom and modern life, showing how to live as enlightened beings in contemporary society.

## How to Approach Goldsmith's Work

1. **Start with *The Infinite Way*** - It provides the complete foundation
2. **Practice daily meditation** - Goldsmith emphasized this as essential
3. **Study his monthly Letters** - These contain deep practical wisdom
4. **Apply the principles** - The truth must be lived, not just understood
5. **Trust the inner teacher** - Goldsmith always pointed to the Christ within
