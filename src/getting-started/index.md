# Getting Started

Internet privacy and data storage are critical issues that require attention and responsibility from individuals and organizations alike. By acknowledging your responsibilities and taking steps to protect personal data, you help promote a culture of data privacy and security, which is essential for protecting individuals and society as a whole from cyber threats and data breaches.

The data you have generated over your lifetime is `a part of you`. You might not recognize it, but your data has become one of your most valuable assets.

From the moment you started using computers and mobile devices, you have been generating information and triggering events that combine to create a digital fingerprint of your life. Do you know where your fingerprints are? Or who has been recording your thoughts and actions?

**Do you have access to, and the ability to benefit from, your own digital fingerprints?**

> Your entire life is valuable.
> — [David Allen](https://gettingthingsdone.com/)
